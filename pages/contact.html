Common Questions

How are service providers selected?

Service providers on Servio are businesses and professionals from the local community. We review basic business information and encourage customer reviews to help maintain service quality. As we grow, we'll implement additional verification processes.

How does Servio work?

Servio connects you directly with local service providers. Browse services, view provider profiles, and book services instantly - all without any commission fees.

How much does it cost to use Servio?

Servio is completely free for customers to use. We don't charge any booking fees or commissions - you only pay for the service directly to the provider.

How do I become a service provider?

Click on "Become a Provider" to start the registration process. You'll need to provide your business details, licenses, and complete our verification process.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve Airdrie and surrounding areas including Calgary and Crossfield. We're continuously expanding to new locations.

What if I'm not satisfied with a service?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. Contact us immediately and we'll work with the service provider to resolve any issues or concerns.
